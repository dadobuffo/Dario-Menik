ABOUT ME

I'm Dario Menichetti, sound designer and audio engineer.

What started as a passion, in 2020 became Artifacts Recordings, a post-production studio where I work with artists to turn their ideas into polished songs through recording, mixing and music production.

In the past few years, I began my journey into game audio, working with indie teams as a composer and sound designer. I developed a passion for interactive audio, I started studying and working with audio middleware and game engines, trying to implement audio through scripting and coding.

With a BA in Music Production, whether it's sculpting sounds for a game or fine-tuning a mix, I always try to use my technical background in an original and creative way.

Let's create something together!
